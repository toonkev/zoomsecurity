9. Zoom Baming (Zoom Bombardment)

If you know the meeting number, you can upload a shocking image or make an irritating sound on audio using file-sharing photos for public meetings. FBI warned. The host of Zoom meetings can mute or drive out troublemakers, but can return straight with a new ID. The best way to avoid Zoom Explosions is to not share Zoom Meeting numbers except for allowed users. Participants will also request the use of a password when logging in to the meeting. - Unsolved.
