1. Account hijacking

Anyone can easily intercept an existing Zoom account if the account email address is known or successfully assumed. If an attacker creates an account with the email address attacker@companyname.com and checks with the bug, the attacker can view all emails generated by *@companame.com in the Zoom app of the company's contacts, allowing the attacker to hack into all accounts in the company. - Corrected.
