7. Security glitches in the meeting waiting room

Zoom advises meeting hosts to set up "standby rooms" to avoid "zoom baming." The waiting room basically holds the participants up until the host lets them in, one at a time or one at a time. Citizens' Research Institute said it found serious security problems in Zoom's waiting room and advised hosts and participants not to use them for the time being. The Citizens' Institute has yet to disclose details, but it has informed Zoom of the glitch. - Corrected.
